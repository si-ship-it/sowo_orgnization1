Home

Welcome to SOWO, the Social Welfare Organization dedicated to transforming lives and communities across Sri Lanka. "SOWO" is a non-profit organization based in Sri Lanka, dedicated to providing support and services to those in need to improve their lives. Our goal is to uplift society through various social work initiatives.

Vision

"Happy Sri Lanka" By empowering individuals, promoting equality, ensuring health and well-being, providing quality education, supporting economic stability, protecting the environment, advocating for social justice, preserving culture, and fostering innovation, SOWO aims to create a fulfilled and prosperous society.

Mission

"Empowering society through Social work" SOWO aims to create a "Happy Sri Lanka" through education, health, community development, disaster relief, economic opportunities, environmental conservation, social advocacy, cultural preservation, and volunteer mobilization.

Services and Programs

  • Educational Programs

  • Community Development

Education Support Volunteers

  • Assist with tutoring sessions, after-school programs, and educational workshops.
  • Requirements: Background in education or tutoring experience preferred.

Health Outreach Volunteers

  • Support health education programs, organize health fairs, and assist with medical outreach.
  • Requirements: Medical or health background is a plus.

Community Engagement Volunteers

  • Engage with local communities, organize events, and promote community activities.
  • Requirements: Strong communication skills and community experience preferred.

Events & Fundraising Volunteers

  • Help plan and execute fundraising events, manage event logistics, and outreach to sponsors.
  • Requirements: Event planning experience or interest in fundraising.
